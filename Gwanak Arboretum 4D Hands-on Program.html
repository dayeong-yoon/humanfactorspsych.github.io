Gwanak Arboretum 4D Program

  • 2019.10.21 - 2019.10.31

  • Gwanak Arboretum
    SNU Human Factors Psychology Lab
    SNU Human Interface Systems Lab
    SNU Human-Centered Computing Lab
    Korea Creative Content Agency
  • With collaborative effort with various departments and government organizations, we were able to conduct a 4D Hands-On Experience Program in Gwanak Arboretum. Utilizing latest technology, we created a unique program integrating peaceful vibe of the Arboretum and human emotion.
Description :

Example paragraph: Collaborating with different labs and a government organization, we were able to create an unique program integrating Gwanak Arboretum's forestry assets and human emotion. The program included the following:


My Unique Forest: Draw an animal of participants' choice and enjoy it being displayed in a forest scenery.

Emotion Tree: Using Facial Recognition Tech, AI recognizes participant's emotion through facial expression and metaphorically translates it into a shape of a tree.

EmotionMap: Take a selfie with a QR code while enjoying the scenery of the Arboretum. Send the photo to a chatroom, then [Emobot] translates your emotion on spot and displays it on the arboretum map.

Arboretum in a different perspective Control the camera on a flying drone, and on a RC car and enjoy the scenery of the Arboretum in a bird and an ant's eyes respectively.

Stress Measurement: Take a stress measurement test and find out how your stress level changed through out the program.


Through this project we were able to establish two SCI dissertations, and a patent about emotion tracking technology.
