Project detail

2021 Cognitive Science Society

  • 2023.02
Description :

Exciting advancements are being made in the field of AI, particularly in open-domain conversation with the introduction of ChatGPT. Our lab is at the forefront of this cutting-edge research, dedicated to pushing the boundaries of AI and creating conversational AIs that are not only coherent, but also engaging and attractive.

We are thrilled to announce the recent accomplishment of Yoon Kyung Lee, a Ph.D. candidate who has created a groundbreaking open-source dataset for conversational AI - OPELA (Open-domain conversations by Personas with Empathy, Long-term memory, and Attractive personality). By training empathy and perspective-taking, such as Theory-of-Mind, Yoon Kyung Lee has made significant progress in making AIs more socially intelligent.


With our collaborators at Seoul National University and Smilegate AI, we have created a high-quality dataset that can improve the quality and accuracy of conversational AI. Our efforts have been recognized by major AI news outlets, Hankyung IT & Science underscoring the importance of our contribution to the research community. The OPELA dataset is now available on this GitHub repository, making it accessible to researchers worldwide.

At our lab, we are committed to remaining at the forefront of AI research and development. We believe that our work will not only accelerate progress in this exciting field but also revolutionize the way we interact with AI. Join us in shaping the future of AI - stay tuned for more updates on our progress and efforts!
